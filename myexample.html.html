Anjuman-I-Islam Kalsekar Technical Campus (AIKTC), New Panvel

 Our Mission:

1) To reach the vision by building on the traditions of Innovation, Problem solving and Multidisciplinary collaboration.
2) To develop competent and confident students for the pharmaceutical industries and health care systems through appropriate teaching-learning process.
3) To impart necessary skill to the students to make them industry/entrepreneurship ready professionals.
4) To sensitize the faculty and students to participate in various competitions and collaborative research projects.
5) To connect, collaborate and assist for various healthcare programs undertaken by professional organizations for the societal benefits.
6) To provide educational experiences that develop students’ knowledge areas, abilities and qualities to enable them to identify needs and construct effective solutions in an economically, socially and culturally relevant manner.
